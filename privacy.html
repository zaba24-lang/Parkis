Privacy Policy — Parkis

Last updated: 2026-01-30

Overview

Parkis helps users interpret parking signs. The app may process images you provide to produce an interpretation.

Data We Process

  • Images: When you choose to scan/select an image, the app processes it to generate results.
  • Diagnostics (optional): If enabled, crash logs/performance data may be collected to improve stability.
  • Push notifications (optional): If you enable notifications, a device token may be used to deliver them.
  • Advertising (optional): If ads are enabled, ad-related data may be processed depending on your ad settings.

Tracking

Parkis does not track you across apps and websites unless you explicitly enable personalized advertising features that require tracking permission.

How We Use Data

  • To provide the core functionality (interpretation results).
  • To maintain and improve the app (bug fixes, performance).
  • To deliver notifications (if enabled).
  • To show ads (if enabled).

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Third-party services may be used only to operate app features (e.g., analytics, messaging, ads), if enabled.

Data Retention

Images are processed to generate results. If you enable history/saving, data may be stored on your device or in configured cloud services.

Your Choices

  • You can deny camera/photo permissions and still use other parts of the app.
  • You can disable notifications in iOS settings.
  • You can disable tracking permission if requested (iOS App Tracking Transparency).

Contact
